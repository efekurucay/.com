## Challenges and Learnings

The primary challenge was not technical implementation but **strategic problem identification**. Analyzing a massive ecosystem like Trendyol to pinpoint high-value opportunities within a limited timeframe required a sharp focus on business metrics. Rapidly prototyping five distinct ideas was made possible by leveraging a high-level development platform.

The key learning is the incredible power of bridging business acumen with rapid development tools. Instead of just talking about an idea, creating a working demo and a clear ROI analysis makes the proposal exponentially more impactful.

## Outcome

The result is a portfolio of five tangible, well-researched, and strategically vital AI project proposals. Each is backed by a business case, a professional presentation, and a live prototype that effectively communicates its value to potential stakeholders. This collection serves as a powerful demonstration of how to apply AI to solve real-world e-commerce challenges.
